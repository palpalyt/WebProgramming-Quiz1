Hometown: Jubail, Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia's Industrial Heartland

Overview

Jubail is located on the Persian Gulf and blends seaside lifestyle with industrial sites. It is actually the largest industrial city in the world and exports a lot of material.

Quick Facts

  • Region: Eastern Province
  • Known for: Fanateer Area, Industrial District
  • Climate: Hot desert; breezy coastline
