Local Food from Jubail

Sample dishes with short descriptions, price range, and where to try.

Kabsah

Fragrant spiced rice with meat, often shared family-style.

  • Price: SAR 25–45
  • Where: Local restaurants near Fanateer

Shawarma

Slow-roasted meat shaved into wraps; quick and popular.

  • Price: SAR 8–15
  • Where: Street kiosks or malls

Luqaimat

Crispy sweet dumplings typically served with date syrup.

  • Price: SAR 10–18
  • Where: Dessert shops and cafés
